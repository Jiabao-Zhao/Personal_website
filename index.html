    Biography

    Jiabao Zhao

    Jiabao Zhao is a Penn State mechanical engineer focused on robotics, AI-enabled robotic agents, computer vision, and human-robot collaborative assembly. His work connects UR5e robot control, RTDE communication, LLM/VLM reasoning, adaptive perception, and practical manufacturing workflows.

    Across research, autonomous systems, and capstone engineering, Jiabao works at the point where mechanical design, sensing, planning, and reliable robot behavior have to meet in the real world.
