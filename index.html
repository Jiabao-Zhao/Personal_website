Biography

Mechanical engineer working where robots meet real human instructions.

My current work focuses on human-robot collaborative assembly, UR5e robotic systems, RTDE control, LLM/VLM-enabled robotic agents, computer vision, and adaptive task planning. I like problems that require both engineering discipline and practical imagination: sensing the environment, reasoning about the task, and turning that reasoning into reliable robot behavior.

I also bring industry and leadership range from PennDOT automated driving systems, Flowserve hydraulic testing, entrepreneurship, and student community leadership.
